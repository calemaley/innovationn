# **App Name**: InnovateHub

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Homepage Display: Showcase the application's core mission, introductory content, and key calls to action for users.
- Intuitive Navigation Bar: Provide a persistent navigation bar for easy access to various sections like Home, Events, Case Studies, Our Partners, and Our Marketplace.
- Speaker Opportunity Form: Allow innovators to submit their interest in speaking to a national audience through a dedicated submission form.
- AI-Powered Topic Suggester: A tool that uses generative AI to suggest relevant presentation topics and keywords for innovators, based on their expertise and current industry trends.
- Static Event Listings: Display a curated, non-interactive list of upcoming innovation-focused events, providing essential details for each.
- Partner Showcase: Present information and branding for key partner organizations in a dedicated section.

## Style Guidelines:

- The brand concept revolves around forward-thinking and professional innovation. This suggests a color palette that is modern and sophisticated, with a touch of vibrance to convey dynamism. The primary color will be a deep, refined blue, hinting at technology and trust. Primary: RGB(10, 63, 204) #0A3FCC.
- For a clean and contemporary feel, the background is a very light, desaturated blue. This ensures readability while subtly echoing the primary hue. Background: RGB(243, 246, 252) #F3F6FC.
- An analogous, slightly brighter violet hue is chosen as an accent color to provide a distinct contrast without clashing, offering an element of creativity and modern flair. Accent: RGB(142, 102, 255) #8E66FF.
- Body font: 'DM Sans' (sans-serif) for its modern, highly legible quality. Headline font: 'DM Serif Display' (serif) to provide an elegant, distinctive contrast, especially for titles and the logo. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use minimalist line-art icons that maintain a clean, sophisticated, and slightly tech-inspired aesthetic, complementing the overall modern design.
- Employ a responsive design with generous spacing and a clear content hierarchy, utilizing a modern grid system for balanced presentation of information across various screen sizes. Padding of `28px 60px` in header and cta rows implies a need for a sense of space around UI elements.
- Incorporate subtle `fadeUp` and `fadeIn` animations for elements like text blocks and images upon load, ensuring a smooth and engaging user experience without being distracting.
